Why do I love Phad See Ew?!

Why am I so obsessed with Phad See Ew?

There's just something about the flavor. Especially after you add the wide noodles and the sauce! And then you mix in those veggies and seafood (which is not cheap, by the way!)--that to me is the ultimate comfort food. Any chance I could drag myself to a Thai restaurant, I would make sure I order it.

The obsession begins...

I first tried it during my undergrad studies. I would always pair it with a thai tea. That was always the best way to go. Whenever I had a bad day, Phad See Ew was the way to go for me. Every time I was in class, Phad See Ew was on my mind. A lot.
